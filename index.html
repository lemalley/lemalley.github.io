A Brief Background

I am a one who has long suffered from the desperate need for perfection. Straight lines, clean layout, eveningly spaced text- these have always been goals to acheive in school posters and projects since elementary school. I always wanted everything to look as professional as possible. However, it wasn't until I had finished my college years and my achedemic goals had been acheived that I was developed the confidence to pursue my curiousity of learning Adobe's Illusrator or InDesign programs. With the help of my youger brother (no shame!), I got my feet wet. It began with a specific project (as most of my challenges have arisen).

I was putting together a reference book for a dear friend of mine's birthday, but it needed a professional cover!
